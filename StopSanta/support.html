Frequently Asked Questions

The game is crashing or not loading.

Please try the following:

  • Make sure your device is running the latest version of its operating system.
  • Make sure you have the latest version of Stop Santa installed.
  • Restart the app by closing it completely and reopening it.
  • Restart your device.

If the issue persists, please contact us with details about your device and the problem.
